Iliana Papanikolaou

Architect · Artist · Creative Developer

Based between Copenhagen, Denmark and Athens, Greece.

My work spans interactive installations, data visualization, music and audio projects, architectural research, editorial design, and video art across multiple scales and mediums. I integrate interdisciplinary methodologies with technical tools to develop bespoke software solutions.

I hold an MArch from Aristotle University and an MSc in Architectural Computation from The Bartlett School of Architecture, UCL.

Previously at Henning Larsen studio in Copenhagen, where I worked on R&D for spatial experiences with a focus on community engagement and inclusivity. I have also held teaching positions in computational media.

Current interests: open-source software development, game engines, 3D sculpting, creative coding.
